My Web Development Journey with eHA Academy

Introduction

Hi there! My name is Samson Ugochukwu Joseph (SUJ), and I’m thrilled to share my journey into the world of web development with you.
As someone who has always been fascinated by technology, I decided to take the plunge and learn how to build websites and applications.

Purpose of the Blog

In this blog, I’ll document my experiences, the challenges I’ve faced, and the skills I’ve gained while learning at eHA Academy.
Whether you’re a beginner or someone considering a career change, I hope my story inspires you to embark on your own journey in web development.

eHA Academy in Summary

eHA Academy offers a comprehensive curriculum covering everything from HTML and CSS to advanced JavaScript frameworks.
The structured approach and supportive community made it the perfect choice for me. When I first logged into the eHA Academy platform, I was greeted with a user-friendly interface and a wealth of resources. I felt a mix of excitement and nervousness as I prepared to dive into my first lesson.


The online platform is designed for ease of use, featuring interactive coding exercises and video tutorials. The community forums are bustling with activity, allowing students to ask questions and share insights.
